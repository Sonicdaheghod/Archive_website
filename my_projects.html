Reisolating Natural Products from Fungal Extracts

March - July 2023

As a undergraduate researcher in the Baker Lab, I extracted four potential natural products (NPs) from a fungal bag that was bioactive against E. faecium, one of the ESKAPE pathogens. The ESKAPE pathogens infect many patients in hospital settings and NPs are small compounds used to form drugs to treat infected patients.
